# 关于不知道的几种说法

# 示例

  1. I have no idea.
  • 我不知道。
  1. I haven't a clue.
  • 不太清楚。
  1. My brain doesn't work.
  • 我搞不清楚。
  1. I don't get it.
  • 我不知道。
  1. It beats me.
  • 不知道啊
  1. Not as far as I know.
  • 据我所知没有。
  1. I haven't the faintest idea.
  • 我不太了解。
  1. You guess is as good as mine.
  • 我和你一样不知道。
  1. Good question. I'll find out.
  • 问得好， 我来解决试试。
  1. Let's have a quick brainstorm.
  • 我们一起来头脑风暴一下吧！
  1. I would Like to research it further and get back to you.
  • 等我研究研究告诉你。
  1. Search me, I'm not a mind reader.
  • 不知道，我又不会读心术。
