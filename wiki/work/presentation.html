Presentation - Lession 17

Presentation

Signposting

Saying what is coming

    1. In this part of my presentation, I’d like to tell you about…
    1. So, let me give you the overview

Moving on to the next point

  • This leads directly to the next part of my talk.
    1. This now leads us to my next point
    1. Let’s move on to the next point
    1. Let’s now turn to the next issue.

Indicating the end of section

  • This brings me to end of my second point.
    1. So much for …collecting data (what’s you have just talked about)
    1. So that’s the background…

Referring back

  • As I mentioned before…
    1. As I said earlier, …
  • Let’s go back to what we were discussing earlier.
    1. Let me now come back to what I said earlier.

Summarizing a point

  • I’d like to sum up the main points
  • Let me briefly summarize what I’ve said so far.
